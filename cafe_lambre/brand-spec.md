# Brand Spec — L'ambre

## Color Tokens (OKLch)
- `--bg`: `oklch(14.07% 0.009 50.8)` (#0d0b08) - Deep obsidian base
- `--surface`: `oklch(18% 0.015 50)` - Slightly lighter dark for cards
- `--fg`: `oklch(92.83% 0.017 84.7)` (#f0e8d8) - Cream text
- `--muted`: `oklch(30.4% 0.04 125)` (#3d402b) - Muted olive accents
- `--border`: `oklch(25% 0.02 60)` - Subtle warm border
- `--accent`: `oklch(64.12% 0.147 75.83)` (#c8852a) - Warm amber highlight

## Typography
- **Display**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif (Elegant, oversized)
- **Body**: 'DM Sans', sans-serif (Clean utility)
- **Accent/Handwritten**: 'La Belle Aurore', cursive (Architectural scrawl)

## Layout Posture
- Full-viewport hero with dramatic staggered entry
- Asymmetric masonry grids for menu sections
- Frosted glass (glassmorphism) for CTA and badges
- Scroll-driven animations (counters, reveals, background shifts)
- Hairline borders (1px) for amber elements
