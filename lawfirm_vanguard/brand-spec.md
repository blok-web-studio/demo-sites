# Brand Specification: VANGUARD

## Visual Posture
- **Style:** Brutalist-meets-editorial.
- **Atmosphere:** Cold, authoritative, sharp.
- **Constraints:** No gradients, flat design, ultra-thin lines, sharp corners.
- **Typography contrast:** Massive display type (uppercase) vs. small, elegant body copy.

## Tokens (OKLch)
- `--bg`: `oklch(96% 0.005 85)` (#f4f1ec - Off-white)
- `--fg`: `oklch(22% 0.005 250)` (#1a1a1a - Deep Charcoal)
- `--surface`: `oklch(22% 0.005 250)` (#1a1a1a)
- `--accent`: `oklch(40% 0.1 245)` (#2d4a6b - Cold Steel Blue)
- `--muted`: `oklch(80% 0.005 85)` (Muted grey-ish off-white)
- `--border`: `oklch(22% 0.005 250)` (Deep Charcoal)

## Typography
- **Display:** "Bebas Neue", "Anton", sans-serif (Uppercase, massive, tight tracking)
- **Body:** "Libre Baskerville", serif (Elegant, readable)
- **Contrast:** Fluid scales with `clamp()`.

## Layout Rules
- **Grid:** Ultra-thin lines (0.5px - 1px).
- **Transitions:** Clip-path wipes, grayscale to color bleeds, SVG stroke animations.
- **Elements:** Sharp rectangular tags, no border-radius.
